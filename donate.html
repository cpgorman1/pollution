The EDF (Environmental Defense Fund) works to address environmental issues that affect people around the world on a daily basis. These issues focus on topics such as the climate, oceans, ecosystems, pollution, and health.

The NRDC (Natural Resources Defense Council) works to sustain the environment for the generations to come as it has for previous generations. The NRDC focuses on topics such as climate change, communities, energy, food, health, oceans, water, and the wild.

“Protecting the lands and waters on which all life depends”, the Nature Conservancy serves to preserve our environment to ensure future success, focusing particularly on lands, waters, oceans, cities, and climate.
